SANE

Sane is a quality product available to everyone. It is often compared to the Canadian brand The Ordinary: behind the minimalist design and low price, effective formulas with active ingredients are hidden. The Sane landmark is a help to those who want to improve the condition of problematic skin. Sane's mission is very simple - a quality product available to everyone.
Sane is a cruelty-free brand: cosmetics are not tested on animals. Products are tested by the in vitro method (in a test tube) and with the help of focus groups.
