SANE

Sane is a quality product available to everyone.

Sane is a cruelty-free brand: cosmetics are not tested on animals. Products are tested by the in vitro method (in a test tube) and with the help of focus groups.

NATUREAL

NATUREAL is a Ukrainian brand of natural cosmetics founded by two sisters, covers the main stages of facial skin care (cleansing, toning, and moisturizing). The brand also offers body products and hair products (solid and classic shampoos, masks, balms, and scalp care). The components that make up the products undergo exclusively biological processes: fermentation, distillation, and cold pressing. 70% of the ingredients are certified by COSMOS / ECOCERT. Each product contains a minimum of 80% highly active ingredients and 40% organic components.
NATUREAL is a cruelty-free brand: no animal testing.
